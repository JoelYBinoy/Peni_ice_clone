ABOUT

WE ARE PENI ICE CANDY

At Peni Ice Candy, we don't just sell frozen treats – we craft
delightful memories that transport you back to your cherished
school days. Our humble beginnings as a local ice candy
business have grown into a heartwarming journey that has
touched the lives of countless individuals throughout Kerala.
With over 10 branches spread across the region, we take pride in
being a part of your sweetest moments.
Remember the thrill of hearing the ice cream vendor's bell
ringing as you rushed out of your classroom during the hot
school days? Our flavors are designed to bring back those very
moments! From classics like mango, orange, and cola, to
creamy indulgences like vanilla milk and chocolate cream, our
diverse range of flavors promises to cater to every palate. Each
bite is a trip down memory lane, a burst of taste that warms your
heart.
